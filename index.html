Bon Appétit Focaccia

    RECIPE
  • Focaccia
    • 2¼ tsp. active dry yeast
    • 2 tsp. honey
    • 2½ cups lukewarm water
    • 5 cups all-purpose flour
    • 5 tsp. Diamond Crystal or 1 Tbsp. Morton kosher salt
    • 6 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
    • Butter for pan
    • Flaky sea salt
  • Brine
    • 1/3 cups water
    • 1½ tsp. salt
  • Garlic Butter
    • 2–4 garlic cloves
    • 4 Tbsp butter
    • Rosemary
    INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Whisk together the yeast and honey in the 2½ cups lukewarm water. Let it sit for 5 minutes and it should foam or get creamy, if not the yeast is dead.
  2. Add the flour and salt, then mix with a rubber spatula until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Pour 4 tbsp. of olive oil into a large bowl. Transfer the dough into the bowl and turn it to coat it in the oil. Then cover the bowl securely and let it chill in the fridge until it doubles in size, about 8-24 hours.
  4. Butter a 13x9 or 18x13 baking pan. Pour 1 tbsp. olive oil into the center of the pan. Keeping the dough in the bowl and using a fork in each hand, gather up edges of dough farthest from you and lift up and over into center of bowl. Give the bowl a quarter turn and repeat process. Do this 2 more times; you want to deflate dough while you form it into a rough ball. Put the dough into the pan and pour any leftover oil and turn dough to coat. Then let the dough rise in a warm place until doubled in size, between at least 1½ to 4 hours.
  5. Make the brine by mixing the 1/3 cups water and the 1½ tsp. salt.
  6. Place a rack in the middle of the oven, preheat to 450°. Lightly oil your hands and then stretch out dough to fill the pan. Dimple the Focaccia with your fingers aggressively, your fingers should reach the bottom of the pan. Drizzle the remaining olive oil, flaky salt, and brine(feel free to only use a little). Then bake focaccia for about 20-30 minutes, check the bottom of the pan when it it seems cooked to avoid burning.
  7. When you're ready to serve the focaccia it's time to start the garlic butter. Melt the butter over medium heat, remove from heat and grate in the garlic, return to heat and cook stirring often for 30-45 seconds.
  8. Brush with garlic butter and slice into squares and rectangles
